Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Furina Bot processes and stores information while providing Discord server features.

1. Information We Process

Furina Bot may process the following information when required for its features:

  • Discord user, server, channel, role, and message IDs.
  • Moderation records such as warnings, moderation cases, temporary punishments, and related reasons.
  • Reminder entries created by users.
  • Server configuration and feature settings.
  • Temporary role information.
  • Presence and activity information used in real time for status and activity commands.
  • Message content only when required for enabled features such as anti-spam, AFK handling, moderation utilities, or bot interactions.

The bot does not intentionally collect Discord passwords, payment information, or private account credentials.

2. How Information Is Used

Information is used only to operate bot features, including moderation, role management, anti-spam protection, AFK handling, reminders, logging, server configuration, presence statistics, utility commands, and interactive features.

3. Storage and Hosting

Data required for the bot to function may be stored on infrastructure provided through Wispbyte or other hosting services used by the bot owner. Stored information may include moderation records, warnings, reminders, server settings, AFK entries, and temporary role data.

Presence information is processed in real time and is not intended to be stored as a historical activity profile.

4. Retention

Some operational records may be retained for longer than 30 days when needed for moderation history, server configuration, or unresolved reminders. Data may be deleted earlier when it is no longer needed, when a server administrator removes it, or after a valid deletion request.

5. Message Content

Message content is processed only when necessary for bot functionality. It is not sold, used for advertising, or used to train machine-learning or artificial-intelligence models.

6. Data Sharing

Furina Bot does not sell personal data. Information may be disclosed only when required by law, necessary to protect users or the service, or required by infrastructure providers to operate the bot.

7. Data Deletion Requests

Users may request deletion of stored data associated with their Discord account by contacting the bot owner through the official support server or the contact method published with the bot. Please include your Discord user ID so the request can be verified and processed.

Server administrators may also request deletion of data associated with their server.

8. Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect stored information. No system is completely secure, so absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

9. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated when the bot, its data practices, or applicable requirements change. The updated date at the top of this page will reflect the latest revision.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact the bot owner through the official Furina Bot support server or the public contact method listed on the bot's application page.
